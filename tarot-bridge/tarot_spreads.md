# Tarot Spreads Comprehensive Guide

Tarot spreads are intentional arrangements of cards used in a reading. Each position in a spread is assigned a specific meaning to provide insights into questions and life situations. The choice of spread depends on the question asked and the depth of insight sought.

Here is a comprehensive list of 20 tarot spreads, along with their layouts and links to view their visual formations.

---

## Basic Spreads (Beginner-Friendly)

### 1. One-Card Spread

- **Purpose**: Quick insights, daily guidance, or answering a focused, straightforward question. Represents a single core message or energy.
- **Layout**: A single card placed in the center.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=One+Card+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 2. Three-Card Spread

- **Purpose**: A versatile and popular spread for quick yet structured insights.
- **Layout**: Three cards laid out horizontally side-by-side (1 - 2 - 3).
  - Common variations include: *Past, Present, Future*, *Situation, Action, Outcome*, or *Mind, Body, Spirit*.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Three+Card+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 3. Five-Card Spread (Linear)

- **Purpose**: Offers a more in-depth analysis of a situation, useful for understanding a timeline or evaluating different facets of a question.
- **Layout**: Five cards placed horizontally in a line.
  - e.g., 1. Past, 2. Present, 3. Future, 4. Underlying cause, 5. Potential outcome.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Five+Card+Linear+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 4. Self Awareness Spread

- **Purpose**: A simple spread for gaining insights into current thoughts and internal feelings.
- **Layout**: Three cards formed in a small triangle (one on top, two on bottom).
  - 1. Mind (Current thoughts), 2. Heart (Current emotions), 3. Spirit (Core self/subconscious).
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Self+Awareness+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

## Intermediate Spreads

### 5. Horseshoe Spread (Seven Cards)

- **Purpose**: Provides a balanced overview of past influences, current circumstances, and future possibilities. Effective for decision-making.
- **Layout**: Seven cards arranged in a semi-circle or "horseshoe" shape, starting from the bottom left, rising to the top center, and falling to the bottom right.
  - 1. Past, 2. Present, 3. Hidden influences, 4. You (Center/Top), 5. External environment, 6. Advice, 7. Outcome.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Horseshoe+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 6. Five-Card Cross Spread

- **Purpose**: Good for problem resolution and exploring the root cause of an issue.
- **Layout**: Arranged in a cross shape.
  - Center (1: Core issue), Left (2: Past), Right (3: Future), Bottom (4: Root cause), Top (5: Potential outcome).
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Five+Card+Cross+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 7. Decision Making Spread (Making Tough Choices)

- **Purpose**: Specifically designed to aid in making difficult decisions between two paths.
- **Layout**: Five cards forming a "Y" or split path.
  - Center bottom (1: Current situation), Top Left branch (2: Option A, 3: Outcome A), Top Right branch (4: Option B, 5: Outcome B).
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Decision+Making+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 8. Catching Feelings Spread

- **Purpose**: Used to understand how someone feels about you or a romantic interest.
- **Layout**: Six cards. A diagonal line of five cards representing your energy and their energy, with one card laid parallel to summarize the root emotion.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Catching+Feelings+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 9. Relationship Spread (The Mirror)

- **Purpose**: To analyze the dynamics of a relationship and how two individuals interact or view each other.
- **Layout**: Two columns facing each other. Column A (Cards 1-3) represents Person A's views/feelings. Column B (Cards 4-6) represents Person B. A central card (Card 7) ties them together as the relationship itself.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Relationship+Mirror+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 10. Relationship Check-In Spread

- **Purpose**: To delve into the past of an established relationship to understand its future trajectory.
- **Layout**: Eight cards. Three cards forming an "L" (The past foundation), two forming a "V" (Current dynamic), and a column of three (Future potential).
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Relationship+Check+In+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

## Advanced Spreads

### 11. Celtic Cross Spread (Ten Cards)

- **Purpose**: One of the most famous and comprehensive spreads, providing a detailed panoramic view of a highly complex situation, issue, or decision.
- **Layout**: A central cross of 6 cards and a staff of 4 cards on the right.
  - Cross: 1. Current State, 2. Obstacle (crosses card 1), 3. Root/Past, 4. Recent Past, 5. Conscious Goal (Above), 6. Near Future.
  - Staff (bottom to top): 7. Yourself, 8. Environments/Others, 9. Hopes/Fears, 10. Ultimate Outcome.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Celtic+Cross+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 12. Astrological Wheel Spread / Zodiac Spread

- **Purpose**: A complex spread offering a comprehensive overview of different life areas based on the 12 astrological houses. Often used for New Year readings or birthdays.
- **Layout**: 12 cards arranged in a large circle representing the 12 houses of the zodiac.
  - E.g., House 1: Self, House 2: Finance, House 4: Home, House 7: Partnerships, House 10: Career, etc.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Astrological+Wheel+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 13. The Three Pyramids (Self-Actualization) Spread

- **Purpose**: Focuses on a person's origins, present state, and ultimate potential.
- **Layout**: Six cards. A main pyramid in the center, flanked by two smaller pyramids (one inverted).
  - Cards 1 & 3: Past influences. Cards 4 & 5: Current self. Card 6: Potential self.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Three+Pyramids+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 14. Akashic Records Spread

- **Purpose**: To explore different facets of your soul, past lives, and life purpose.
- **Layout**: Six or seven cards forming a circle representing the continuous journey of the soul (Past life, current lessons, inner truth, hidden potential, future life).
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Akashic+Records+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 15. Chakra Tarot Spread

- **Purpose**: Used for self-help, healing, and identifying physical, emotional, or spiritual blockages.
- **Layout**: Seven cards laid vertically in a straight line from bottom to top.
  - Card 1: Root Chakra, Card 2: Sacral, Card 3: Solar Plexus, Card 4: Heart, Card 5: Throat, Card 6: Third Eye, Card 7: Crown.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Chakra+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

## Specialized & Timing Spreads

### 16. Full Moon Spread

- **Purpose**: Designed to reflect on what needs to be released or culminated during a full moon phase.
- **Layout**: Four to six cards in a semi-circle resembling the moon's glow. Positions cover: What to release, What has come to fruition, Hidden shadows, and Next steps.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Full+Moon+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 17. New Moon Spread

- **Purpose**: Focuses on setting new intentions, planting seeds, and embracing new beginnings.
- **Layout**: Often shaped like a crescent moon, comprising four to five cards mapping out intentions, required actions, and potential growth.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=New+Moon+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 18. Daily Manifestation Spread

- **Purpose**: Helps in creating a desired reality, keeping focus on daily, weekly, or bigger life goals.
- **Layout**: Usually three to four cards structured as steps on a staircase. 1. Current focus, 2. Blockage, 3. Action step, 4. Manifestation potential.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Manifestation+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 19. The Path Spread

- **Purpose**: Used when feeling lost or seeking direction in life or career.
- **Layout**: Laid out resembling a winding road or stepped path. Explores the past steps taken, the current standing stone, obstacles on the path, and the destination.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=The+Path+Tarot+Spread+Layout)

### 20. My Future Self Spread

- **Purpose**: To connect with and gain insights from a future, more evolved version of yourself.
- **Layout**: Eight or nine cards in three rows (Bottom, Middle, Top). The bottom row represents the current self, the middle row is the transitional bridge, and the top row represents the future self.
- **Visual**: [🔍 View Formation Images](https://www.google.com/search?tbm=isch&q=Future+Self+Tarot+Spread+Layout)
